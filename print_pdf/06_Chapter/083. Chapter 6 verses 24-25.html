Chapter 6 — dhyāna yoga

083. Chapter 6 verses 24-25

Lectures by Swami Sarvapriyananda
Verse 6.24-25
सङ्कल्पप्रभवान्कामांस्त्यक्त्वा सर्वानशेषत: ।
मनसैवेन्द्रियग्रामं विनियम्य समन्तत: ।। 24।।
saṅkalpaprabhavānkāmāṃs
tyaktvā sarvānaśeṣataḥ |
manasaivendriyagrāmaṃ
viniyamya samantataḥ ||
◈ saṅkalpa-prabhavān – born of imagination/willful thought; kāmān – desires; ◈ tyaktvā – having abandoned; sarvān – all; aśeṣataḥ – completely; ◈ manasā – by the mind; eva – alone; indriya-grāmam – the whole group of senses; ◈ viniyamya – restraining; samantataḥ – from all sides.
Abandoning completely all desires born of 'Sankalpa' (thought/imagination), and restraining the whole group of senses from all sides by the mind alone,
